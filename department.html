About the Department

Our Department of Engineering & Technology stands at the forefront of innovation and academic excellence, dedicated to shaping the next generation of engineers and technology leaders.

With a vision to advance technological innovation and foster critical thinking, our mission is to provide world-class education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.

We offer cutting-edge programs in Electronics, Computer Science, Chemical Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence, each designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills and deep technical knowledge.

Our programs are important because they bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application, ensuring graduates are ready to lead innovation in their respective fields and contribute meaningfully to society's technological advancement.

Through state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, we create an environment where curiosity meets opportunity, and passion transforms into expertise.
